Top 10 Wellness Tips

  • Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to stay hydrated
  • Include colorful fruits and vegetables in every meal
  • Practice mindful eating - chew slowly and savor your food
  • Get 7-9 hours of quality sleep each night
  • Move your body for at least 30 minutes daily
  • Reduce processed foods and added sugars
  • Practice stress-reduction techniques like meditation
  • Prepare meals at home using whole ingredients
  • Connect with your support community regularly
  • Celebrate small wins and stay consistent
